Sungha Jung

Sungha Jung is a South Korean musician who specializes in acoustic fingerstyle guitar. He has a YouTube channel called jwcfree which has his song covers and compositions. Jung creates acoustic covers and arrangements, typically by ear and/or by watching videos, and has original music, which he plays and uploads online. He started playing since he was 9, now he's 20. Jungha was nicknamed the "Guitar Prodigy" and the "August Rush" in Korea, though he prefers to be known as a 'guitarist' rather than a 'guitar prodigy'.
